Smaug, the Terrible

"My armour is like tenfold shields, my teeth are swords, my claws spears, the shock of my tail is a thunderbolt, my wings a hurricane, and my breath death!"

  1. Race: Dragons
  2. Gender: Male
  3. Eyes: Gold
  4. Culture: Fire-drake

Smaug was a fire drake of the Third Age, considered to be the last "great" dragon to exist in Middle-earth.
